--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TRAINING CONFIGURATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Transfer Learning Strategy (all pretrained models):
    Stage 1 — Freeze base, train classifier head only (LR=0.001, 10 epochs)
    Stage 2 — Unfreeze last 30 layers, fine-tune (LR=0.0001, up to 40 epochs)

  Callbacks:
    - EarlyStopping        (patience=8, monitor=val_accuracy)
    - ModelCheckpoint      (save best val_accuracy weights)
    - ReduceLROnPlateau    (factor=0.5, patience=4)

  Class weights applied: Yes (to handle imbalance ratio of 2.16)
  VGG16: Skipped — too slow on CPU (138M parameters)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
OBSERVATIONS & ANALYSIS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  1. Custom CNN outperformed all transfer learning models.
     Likely reasons: small dataset, CPU-limited epochs, simpler model converged faster.

  2. InceptionV3 second best at 83.19% — multi-scale features suit mushroom textures.

  3. DenseNet201 achieved only 73.45% vs 92.5% in reference paper (Wongpanya).
     Reference used GPU with more epochs — GPU retraining recommended.

  4. ResNet50 (67.26%) and EfficientNetB0 (44.25%) underperformed due to
     insufficient fine-tuning epochs on CPU.
